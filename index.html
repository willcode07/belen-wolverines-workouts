Safety first

Before you start

Open this page once on Wi‑Fi, then add it to your Home Screen. After that it works with no signal.

Pick your week → day. Choose Full gym or Floor only.

Odd weeks vs even weeks

Odd weeks (1, 3, 5, 7, 9): learn the moves, keep form clean.

Even weeks (2, 4, 6, 8, 10): new patterns + higher intensity.

Weeks 9–10 are the hardest. Stop a set early if your form breaks.

What is RPE?

RPE = Rate of Perceived Exertion (how hard it feels from 1–10).

6 = warm / easy · 7 = challenging but clean · 8 = hard · 9 = almost max

Match the RPE shown on each day. If form gets sloppy, stop the set.

Tempo tip

3s down = lower for 3 seconds. 2s down/up = 2 seconds each way.

Gym tips

No pain on shoulders — shrink the range.

Sleep well, especially in weeks 9–10.
